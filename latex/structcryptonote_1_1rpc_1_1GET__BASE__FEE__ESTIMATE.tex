\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Gives an estimation of per-\/output + per-\/byte fees

Inputs\+:


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\ttfamily grace\+\_\+blocks} If specified, make sure that the fee is high enough to cover any fee increases in the next {\ttfamily grace\+\_\+blocks} blocks.
\end{DoxyItemize}

Output values available from a public R\+PC endpoint\+:


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\ttfamily status} General R\+PC status string. {\ttfamily \char`\"{}\+O\+K\char`\"{}} means everything looks good.
\item {\ttfamily emission\+\_\+amount} Amount of coinbase reward in atomic units.
\item {\ttfamily fee\+\_\+amount} Amount of fees in atomic units.
\item {\ttfamily burn\+\_\+amount} Amount of burnt oxen.
\item {\ttfamily fee\+\_\+per\+\_\+byte} Amount of fees estimated per byte in atomic units
\item {\ttfamily fee\+\_\+per\+\_\+output} Amount of fees per output generated by the tx (adds to the {\ttfamily fee\+\_\+per\+\_\+byte} per-\/byte value)
\item {\ttfamily blink\+\_\+fee\+\_\+per\+\_\+byte} Value for sending a blink. The portion of the overall blink fee above the overall base fee is burned.
\item {\ttfamily blink\+\_\+fee\+\_\+per\+\_\+output} Value for sending a blink. The portion of the overall blink fee above the overall base fee is burned.
\item {\ttfamily blink\+\_\+fee\+\_\+fixed} Fixed blink fee in addition to the per-\/output and per-\/byte amounts. The portion of the overall blink fee above the overall base fee is burned.
\item {\ttfamily quantization\+\_\+mask} 
\end{DoxyItemize}
