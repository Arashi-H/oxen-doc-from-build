\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Returned type of parse\+\_\+incoming\+\_\+txs() that provides details about which transactions failed and why. This is passed on to handle\+\_\+parsed\+\_\+txs() (potentially after modification such as setting {\ttfamily approved\+\_\+blink}) to handle\+\_\+parsed\+\_\+txs() to actually insert the transactions.
