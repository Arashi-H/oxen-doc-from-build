\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Mnemonic seed generation and wallet restoration from them. 

This file and its cpp file are for translating Electrum-\/style word lists into their equivalent byte representations for cross-\/compatibility with that method of \char`\"{}backing up\char`\"{} one\textquotesingle{}s wallet keys.
