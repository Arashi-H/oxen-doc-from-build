Detailed Description

Get all accounts for a wallet. Optionally filter accounts by tag.

Inputs:

  • tag – (Optional) Tag for filtering accounts. All accounts if empty, otherwise those accounts with this tag
  • strict_balances – If true, only return the balance for transactions that have been spent and are not pending (i.e. excluding any transactions sitting in the TX pool)

Outputs:

  • total_balance – Total balance of the selected accounts (locked or unlocked).
  • total_unlocked_balance – Total unlocked balance of the selected accounts.
  • subaddress_accounts – Account information.
    • account_index – Index of the account.
    • base_address – The first address of the account (i.e. the primary address).
    • balance – Balance of the account (locked or unlocked).
    • unlocked_balance – Unlocked balance for the account.
    • label – (Optional) Label of the account.
    • tag – (Optional) Tag for filtering accounts.
