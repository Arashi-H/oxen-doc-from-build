uint64_t cryptonote::block_reward_parts::original_base_reward

The base block reward from which non-miner amounts (i.e. SN rewards and governance fees) are calculated. Before HF 13 this was (mistakenly) reduced by the block size penalty for exceeding the median block size; starting in HF 13 the miner pays the full penalty.
